The Best Cookie Recipe

This recipe for chocolate chip cookies is to die for! They come out of the oven all ooey and gooey and oh so good.

For this recipe, you'll need a couple of ingriedients. Such as:

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • Chocolate Chips

And that's it! Now, how to make these cookies? Well, it's really quite simple!

  1. First, Beat the sugar and butter together.
  2. Next, Add the eggs and a teaspoon of salt and mix until it's all combined.
  3. Third, Add in the flour and baking powder and mix until it is all combined.
  4. Fourth, add in the chocolate chips and mix them evenlu throughout the dough.
  5. Next, line a baking pan with parchament paper and add cookie dough balls to the sheet.
  6. Lastly, bake at 350* Farenheit for 9 minutes. Let cool before serving. This recipe makes 10-12 cookies.

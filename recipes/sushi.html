How to Bring Asia to Your Home

Fresh sushi is one of the best meals you can have. But with the prices being so expensive, there have to be cheaper but tasty options, right? Well yes. there are. By making it at home, you are keeping the cost as low as possible while having a tasty meal that will feel like it came straight from a sushi bar.

Sushi is one of the easiest meals you can make at home. You only need 4 ingridients:

  • Seaweed Sheets
  • Preferred type of Fish
  • Rice
  • Toppings

Yep! That's really all you need to create fire sushi. The steps are even easier to make them!:

  1. First, lay the seaweed sheet on a counter or table.
  2. Next, add rice and spread evenly on top of the seaweed, covering the whole sheet.
  3. Third, add on the fish, cooked or raw, in the middle of the sheet in a line.
  4. Lastly, add on any other topping you like and roll it up. Cut into equally sized circles and enjoy!
